Aloo Dum Biryani

Aloo dum biryani is one of the famous Biryanis of Hyderabad. Deep Fried potato and deep fried chicken are added to Biryani. This Biryani has a scrumptious taste. You can add the potato as per your choice; one is to add boiled potatoes, and other is to add deep fried potatoes.
