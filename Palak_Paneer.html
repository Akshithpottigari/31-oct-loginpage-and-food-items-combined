Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is one of the most popular Indian dish made with succulent paneer cubes (Indian cottage cheese) in a smooth spinach sauce. This vibrant green palak paneer is made with fresh spinach leaves (palak in Hindi), onions, tomatoes, herbs and spices.
